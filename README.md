# Interactive Neume
A fully interactive virtual Neume player you can play with your **Computer Keyboard** or **Mouse-Click/Touch Support.**
<br>This virtual player is great for learning to sound out notes for Neume Notation and for Gregorian Chants.</br>
It does **NOT** playback Neume music sheets. The primary purpose for this player is to discern sound on a neume sheet.

## How to Play
<br>Simply open the HTML file in your browser (Chrome, Firefox, etc.)</br>
**Right Click on "Interactive-Neume.html" -> Open With -> (Your Browser)**
### Controls
**Computer Keyboard**
- *Q, W, E, R, T, Y, U, I, O, P, '[', ']', '\\'*
- Shift + (any key) = 

<br>**Mouse-Click/Touch**: Click or Touch anywhere on the staff lines.</br>
