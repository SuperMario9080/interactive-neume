# Interactive Neume
A fully interactive virtual Neume player you can play with your **Computer Keyboard** or **Mouse-Click/Touch Support.**

This virtual player is great for learning to sound out notes for Neume Notation and for Gregorian Chants.<br>
It does **NOT** play neume music sheets. The primary purpose of this player is to discern sound on a neume sheet.

## How to Play
Simply open the HTML file in your browser (Chrome, Firefox, etc.)<br>
**Right Click on "Interactive-Neume.html" -> Open With -> (Your Browser)**
### Controls
**Clef Placement:**
- *Hold and Click/Touch* the Clef and Move it Up/Down,

**Computer Keyboard:**
- *Q, W, E, R, T, Y, U, I, O, P, '[', ']', '\\'*
- Shift + (Any Key/Mouse Click/Touch) = Plays a Flat Note (♭)
- *Down/Up Arrow Keys* = -1/+1 Octave
- *Left/Right Arrow Keys* = -1/+1 Transpose

**Mouse-Click/Touch**: Click or Touch anywhere on the staff lines.

## C/F Clefs
*DISCLAIMER: (The symbols aren't accurate and are placeholders for now. I might change it to simply "C" or "F")*

**C Clef**: Placing this on any line becomes The ***Middle C*** or ***(C4)*** of the diatonic scale.<br>
**F Clef**: Placing this on any line becomes the ***'F' note*** of the diatonic scale.
